Technique

Look Ahead

While executing one step, identify your next piece. Top cubers plan 3–4 moves ahead so there's zero hesitation between steps.

Hands

Finger Tricks

Master the R-U flick: right index pushes R up, same finger flicks U clockwise. Practice R U R' U' until it's a single fluid motion.

Efficiency

Minimize Regrips

Every regrip costs ~0.3s. Plan your entire grip for algorithm sequences so your hands never need to stop and reposition.

Practice

Slow is Smooth

Practice algorithms at 50% speed with zero mistakes. Accuracy builds better muscle memory than rushing. Speed follows naturally.

Hardware

Tune Your Cube

Lubricate with silicone spray and adjust spring tension. A well-tuned magnetic speed cube cuts seconds off your time effortlessly.

Memory

Blindfold Algs

Practice algorithms with eyes closed. When your hands know every move by feel, your eyes are completely free to plan what's next.
